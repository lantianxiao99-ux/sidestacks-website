Features

Everything your side hustle needs

Purpose-built for Australians running freelance work, reselling businesses, and multiple income streams.

💰

Income & Expense Tracking

Log every transaction in seconds. Categorise income and expenses across multiple businesses, with receipt photo attachments.

🧾

Invoice Generation

Create professional invoices with GST, ABN, and client details. Generate and share PDFs directly from the app.

🚗

Mileage Tracking

Log business trips and automatically calculate your ATO mileage deduction at $0.85/km, capped at 5,000 km per year.

Tax & BAS Estimates

Know what you owe before tax time. Automatic GST calculations, BAS summaries, and deductible expense tracking.

Analytics Dashboard

Profit trends, client insights, hourly rate analysis, anomaly detection, and goal tracking — all in one live dashboard.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No accountant required. No spreadsheets. Just open the app and go.

1

Create your SideStack

Add each of your businesses or income streams as a separate “stack”. Freelancing, reselling, content — keep them all clean and separate.

2

Log or import transactions

Add transactions manually in seconds, or connect your bank to import them automatically. Attach receipts, add notes, categorise on the fly.

3

Know your numbers

Your profit, tax estimate, GST, and mileage deduction update in real time. No surprises at the end of the financial year.
